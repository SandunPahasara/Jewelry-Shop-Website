Our Heritage

With a design heritage that focus on nearly two decades, Luxe Jewelry has perfected the fine art of themed portrayals. Each piece reflects substance and meaning that go beyond surface beauty.

This characteristic uniqueness is deeply rooted in our design approach; practiced by the craftsmen of the Luxe family who are among the finest in the trade. Everything we do is a labour of passion, and every creation at our hands; a memorable statement of elegance.

Unique Gems

We feature some of the most stunning selections of precious gems, discovered and sourced through the finest purveyors. Taking center stage are our unique sapphires and emeralds.
